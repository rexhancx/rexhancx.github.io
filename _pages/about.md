# About me

I am a master graduate in Department of Animal Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University supervised by Professor He Meng.

My current research focuses on __chicken genome editing using adenoviral Cas9 vector__. I am very open to collaborating with others and have tried other life science field, e.g., cancer research, neroscience.
